About

I was born in Minnesota and have lived and attended school there ever since. Growing up, I appreciated the times when I could be lazy. For example, instead of making my own flashcards to study from, I would just use quizlet. As I encountered these moments, I became more and more fascinated by the power of the web and its influence in my generation.

I am currently a junior at the University of Minnesota Twin Cities pursuing degrees in Computer Science and Mathematics. There I am looking for opportunities to learn and leverage the best practices for software design and development. My experiences outside academia are mainly projects built on web and mobile, but I am always looking to expand my skillset. Currently I am focused on learning the MEAN stack, and dabbling in machine learning.

I like to spend my free time learning new technologies for different platforms, watching popular media (TV shows, movies and anime), playing piano, and trying to stay active. I enjoy outings with friends, but sometimes I can be an introvert. In that case, I like to work on anything that stimulates my brain, be it a small programming challenge or a personal project.
